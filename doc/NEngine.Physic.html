Namespace: Physic

NEngine.Physic

All physic related stuff, structures to index and optimize n-dimensional spaces with lots or hundreds of entities like space trees. It also holds the PhysicModules wich define diferent kinds of physic processors and physic types, and defines the SpaceGraph for easy space configuration (lots of TODO here)

Space and SpaceNode implement an axis oriented topology cappable of optimize them for collisisions and interspace intersections.

Space is the SpaceNode network container, and holds common configurations for every SpaceNode.
SpaceNode is a recursive representation of a n-axis
